Patient Centered Bioinformatics at the Intersection of Cloud Computing and App development

The Planet of the Apps

Jonas S Almeida, Ph.D., Professor & CTO ( jonasalmeida.info)

 

The emergence of the Web Platform as the dominant vehicle for consumer informatics has created opportunities for disseminating research applications at a truly global scale, and in a manner that

  1. a) Persists in time;
  2. b) Requires minimal maintenance;
  3. c) Delivers reproducible analysis, and
  4. d) is much safer than the conventional model where data leaves the original hosting environment.
